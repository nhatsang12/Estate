# Amenity Aliases

This document maps common user-friendly terms and aliases for amenities to their standardized representation in the EstateManager platform's property data.

## Standard Amenities and their Aliases:
- **Hồ bơi (Pool):**
    - pool
    - hồ bơi
- **Phòng gym (Gym):**
    - gym
    - phòng gym
- **WiFi (Wireless Internet):**
    - wifi
    - internet
- **Điều hoà (Air Conditioning):**
    - điều hoà
    - máy lạnh
    - air conditioner
- **Bãi đỗ xe (Parking):**
    - parking
    - bãi đỗ xe
    - chỗ đậu xe
- **Ban công (Balcony):**
    - balcony
    - ban công
- **Bảo vệ 24/7 (24/7 Security):**
    - bảo vệ
    - an ninh
    - security
